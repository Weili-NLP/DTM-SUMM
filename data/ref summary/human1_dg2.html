[1] Sentiment analysis (opinion mining) involves collect and examine opinions about the product made in blog posts, comments, reviews or tweets. [2] Sentiment classification classifies sentiment polarity is positive, negative or neutral on document, sentence, phrase or aspect or attribute level. [3] Aspect-based Sentiment classification considers the sentiments on features or aspects of certain products. [4] The Semantic orientation approach to Sentiment analysis is unsupervised learning. [5] It measures how far a word is inclined towards positive, negative or neutral. [6] The lexicon-based approach depends on finding the opinion lexicon: Bing Liu’s opinion lexicon, IMDB, MPQA, SentiWordNet, NRC Emotion Association lexicon. [7] The machine learning approach applicable to sentiment analysis mostly belongs to supervised classification. [8] Blogs, movie and camera reviews, tweets provide opinions and sentiments to products and services. [9] The opinion holders give different opinions for different aspects of the same entity. [10] A set of domain-specific resources like Sentiment Ontology Tree SOT which capture knowledge in a given domain.
